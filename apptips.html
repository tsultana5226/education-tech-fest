Application Tips

Choosing A School

When you are checking out schools and programs, it is important to consider what will help you learn and grow. Think about your interests, learning needs, and how you will get to school. NYC School Finder The School Finder is the best way to explore high schools and programs. You can find schools based on your interests, where you live, and more all on your mobile device or computer! NYC High School Directory Learn about applying to high school and find programs that interest you in each borough. Pick up a copy of the directory at your middle school or at High School Admissions events.

Applying To Schools

High school applications are given out to 8th graders each fall. Here are a few strategies for submitting a strong application. Start exploring schools and programs the summer between 7th and 8th grade. Find places where you think you might be happy. Looking at 20 to 30 programs is a good starting point. Consider your chances of getting an offer. Balance your application between schools that are a “reach” for you and target schools or programs that seem like a strong match. Learn more about your chances of a match in the NYC High School Directory. List 12 programs on your Round 1 Application—in order of preference. Submit your completed application by the due date, usually early December of each year.
